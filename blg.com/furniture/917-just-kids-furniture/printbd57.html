Just Kids Furniture

Established in 2006 by George Sotiropoulos and his wife Fay, Just Kids Furniture offers the best range of furniture in Australia. Complementing the services with lively and colourful flair, the business is run with great passion. Through the customised furniture, the couple wants to create good memories and intrinsically satisfying masterpieces that suit the personal taste of their clients.

In the business and in their Braeside retail shop, George makes and paints the furniture, while Fay handles the customer services. Simultaneously, both of them creatively focus on designing and creating kids furniture with the highest quality. Along with the collective efforts of the owners, the team at Just Kids Furniture collaboratively works through the whole process of designing, CNC cutting, engineering the parts, construction, painting, sanding, undercoating and top coating. With great expertise, the furniture at Just Kids Furniture is something perfect out of your dreams.

Suitable for a wide range of applications, ranging from the outdoors to the bedroom - we handle all the custom furniture requirements from our clients. Moreover, our design and manufacturing are highly compatible with the Australian Safety standards of Furntech.

Let us transform your space with the best-customised furniture towards long-lasting memories.
