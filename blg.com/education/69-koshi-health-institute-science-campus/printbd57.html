Koshi Health Institute / & Science Campus

Welcome to Koshi Health Institute / & Science Campus Biratnagar,! We are located at the heart of Biratnagar city, opposite to sansarimaistahan. We are the Best Health education center in eastern region of nepal.

Our institution has been established for producing qualified manpower in the sector of health, we are providing certificate level education,since 2055 BS after being affiliated to CTEVT(The Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training) under CTEVT we run diploma in pharmacy (D.PHARMA),HA(Health Assistant),CMA, ANM and Lab Assistant. We are also affiliated to Purwanchal University and offering BPH, BN and B.Sc Nursing Programs.
Vision-
Building Health Futures by increasing the equity, effectiveness and efficiency health services to all populations.
Mission -
To develop next generation of leaders in the health sector by conducting the highest quality research, educating health professions in policy, management and public health practice, and partnering with the community to disseminate and apply new knowledge.

Goal/ Objectives-


-Contribute to meet the great demand of rural health care providers.

-Help to create jobs for rural health.

-Contribute to improve quality and accessibility of primary health care to the people.

-Commitment to realizing our mission by achieving and continually improving our research, education and service goals.
