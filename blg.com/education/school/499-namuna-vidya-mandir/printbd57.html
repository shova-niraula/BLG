Namuna Vidya - Mandir Featured

Namuna Vidya-Mandir (NVM) was established in 2050 B.S. along with a rented building. It has come a long way in the field of both educational and economical standard because it has been able to set up its own new building at Madhumara (Peepal Chowk) and classes from Nursery to Standard Ten are regularly going on. Besides, classes from Nursery to five are also running in Devkota Chowk. Believing that "Knowledge is Power" NVM has also been able to touch the hearts of all by providing imparting quality education and by having 100% SLC result, 2054.
