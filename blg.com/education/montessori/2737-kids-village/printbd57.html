Kids Village Featured

Kids Village, the first time in Biratnagar, is scientifically designed pre school with a real Montessori concept established in 2070 B.S. There has been a gap between the prospective children and good quality pre school education in Biratnagar. A team of academicians therefore, wants to use its expertise to fill up the deficit of motherly treatment behaviour in theory and practice by providing good education at pre school level.
