Shikshadeep Higher Secondary School Featured

Shikshadeep H.S.S. is an educational organization of professionals working together in the field of education for a long time with distinct aim to provide quality education focusing on the career formation of students to make them able and dignified citizens of the 21st century. It is managed by a highly experienced, dedicated and renowned team of SCIENCE and MANAGEMENT faculties committed to ensure bright future to students enrolled in it. As +2 Science and +2 Management are two separate programs, there are two separate blocks for each equipped with all the facilities required for science and management students. Shikshadeep H.S.S. is situated at the heart of Biratnagar Sub-Metropolitan city, ward no. 14, near Neel Sadan, easily accessible from all the parts of the city.
