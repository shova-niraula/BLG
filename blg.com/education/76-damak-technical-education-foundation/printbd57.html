Damak Technical Education Foundation

Damak Technical Education Foundation (DTEF), Damak-10 was established in 2065 B.S. to offer various educational programmes as a part of its effort to bring education to masses and fulfill the growing demand for technical and management professionals. Affiliated to the Tribhuwan University (TU), Sikkim Manipal University (SMU)and Council for Technical Education and Vocation Training (CTEVT), It was founded as a private campus in Damak to fulfill the increasing demand of higher education in the area of technological sector. DTEF is further dedicated and determined to cultivate a purely educational environment that stimulates and encourages a quest and thirst of learning by the best utilization of its various resources/ techniques. The college (DTEF) gives emphasis on innovative and creative teaching-learning activities along with the occasional co-curricular activities. Campus inspires creative learners to find golden path for their glowing future. Thanks !
