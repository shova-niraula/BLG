Applied College

Applied College is established with a distinct view to provide quality education to the students thereby making them globally competent. Applied College is run by young, energetic academicians and professionals of the eastern-region. It is exclusively devoted and dedicated to achieve its goal to produce highly efficient human resources for the contemporary global market. Applied College places equal emphasis on ethical, social and practical values for global knowledge. The term APPLIED derives its name from the 7 basic human values; Attitude, Practice, Planning, Learning, Intelligence, Equality, Distinct. Applied team comprises highly experienced, qualified and leading academic professionals and astute managers with enormous exposure and recognition in the field of management studies. Applied College is promoted by Professional Careers and Education Pvt. Ltd., the college is affiliated to Tribhuwan University for management programme like BBS, CA and AT(Affiliated to ICAN) as well as professional job oriented training. The college is situated at a prime location of Biratnagar.
