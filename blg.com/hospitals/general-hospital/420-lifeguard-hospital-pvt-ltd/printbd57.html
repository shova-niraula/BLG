Lifeguard Hospital Pvt. Ltd.
Life Guard Hospital Pvt. Ltd. referred to as LG started as a Life Guard Diagnosis & Pathology Center in 6 October 2004 (20 Ashoj 2061) with a philanthropic vision of serving the mankind and contributing to the health sector of Nepal. The name itself describe that it has started its operation as a diagnostic center. Life guard hospital is dedicated to the “Patient first” with a team of specialists working together, along with the best technology available today to optimize patient outcome.

The hospital has constructed its own building in the Bargachhi area of Biratnagar and started its operation with Siemens make Double Slice CT Scanner System- SOMATOM Emotion Duo services to the patients in the new premises. We have 6 kattha lands and constructed our state-of-the-art technology building covering more than 30,000 sq. ft.
