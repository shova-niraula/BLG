Da Yatra Courtyard
We are a “Neoclassical Luxury Hotel” in the helm of the tourist hub of Pokhara .69 Kms from lake Fewa. The hotel offers an assortment of 46 rooms with satori evoking views of the Himalayas and lake from lake-view rooms and the terrace. Host meetings and conferences at our conference halls which can accommodate 135-150 people in various formations. Enjoy gourmet food prepared by our chef and kitchen team served by amicable wait-staff. Complimentary self parking is available on site. Hotel Da Yatra has designated areas for smoking. Airport pickup/drop facility and well informed staff to assist you in travel inquiries (tour/ticket assistance).
