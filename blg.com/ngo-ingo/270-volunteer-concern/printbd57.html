Volunteer Concern

Volunteer Concern is a non-profit volunteer organization that focuses on three sides of a society: Empathy, Education and Environment. We run various community development programs within these sectors in Nepal through volunteers around the world. Volunteer Concern basically is a concern to contribute a little from your side in countries like Nepal as a volunteer. Concern here means a meaningful travel as an initiative for positive consequence. Your concern is for support, for awareness, for construction or for the compassion
