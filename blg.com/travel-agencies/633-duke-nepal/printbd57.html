Ghorepani Poonhill Trekking
Ghorepani Poonhill Trekking;An Easy Trek

Ghorepani Poonhill Trekking is a popular trekking route among Nepalese and foreigners too. It is a short and easy adventure around Annapurna region where any individual can have once in a lifetime memories. Stunning Sunrise view , the panorama of Himalayas, natural and cultural importance of place makes this trek extra beautiful. Poonhill view point offers rich view of Annapurna, Dhaulagiri, Nilgiri, Himchuli, Machhapuchre and other Himalaya peaks. Gurungs and Magars are local inhabitants of Ghorepani and this place also offers cultural insight of these communities.
