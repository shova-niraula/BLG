Central Mall Featured

In order to bring back the Glory of the City, 3 prominent business houses, Debenara Group, Vishal group, and Hi-Life group joined hands to develop an International Standard Mall and Multiplex at the Heart of the Biratnagar City, and named it Central Mall, Central Multiplex. Central Mall is designed as the retail redefinition of Biratnagar, an International Standard of shopping & entertainment destination with a built up area of 1 Lakh 34 thousand square feet.



Central Mall is the first premium retail & entertainment destination mall in Biratnagar. It has been designed to complement the changing perspectives and lifestyles of modern-day Biratnagar with state-of-the-art building systems and a superior design balancing the contemporary architecture. It stands as a symbol of positive growth and it is a Multi-utility commercial complex comprising a wide range of luxury Brand Outlets, Department Store, Food Court, Entertainment Zones, 2-Screen Multiplex and Sky lounge & Bar – all come together in the upmarket Main road -7 to offer the epitome of Hospitality not just in Birat but in the state no.1.Central Mall has earned the Destination of being the City's only organized Fashion Destination.
