The Superheroz

THE SUPERHEROZ INCORPORATIONS, the name itself clearly signifies enthusiasm and eagerness to promote our region conglomerates a management team of young blood assembled with one theme and vision to organize events and program that can enhance the beauty and name of our city with full faith. As our city has always been like a dry zone in relation to good events or for a place to be when people want to enjoy and have fun , we the team of The SuperHeroz Inc. thought of organizing programs which in some ways entertain people, make them feel good and hopes to create a oasis of fun and enjoyment.

In the light of social responsibilities we The SuperHeroz Inc. has also thought of organizing some social welfare programs like financial support and encouraging to the needy ones like old aged homes or the orphanage homes as well as awareness programs like global warming, health awareness programs, personality development trainings and much more in the coming future not only fun and enjoyment.

Not letting people forget, we will be focusing more on cultural programs with new ideas and assuring people for no dull moments. Motivating the youths of today and believing in our nation will always be a part of our theme.
