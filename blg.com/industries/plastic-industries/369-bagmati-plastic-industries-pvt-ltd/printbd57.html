Bagmati Plastic Industries (Pvt.)Ltd.

We are a manufacturing unit situated in the Green Industrial Town of Biratnagar, mainly engaged in producing plastic household appliances, hdpe blown jars, pet preform and pet blown items.

Along with that we are manufacturing jerrycanes and containers for a decade now catering to vegetable ghee industries, edible oils and other eatables as well. From four years we have started manufacturing pet preforms and recently added pet blown jars, bottles to our ever growing list of packaging solutions mainly for food items and beverages.

Our group concerns are engaged in manufacturing plastic household furniture, edible oil packaging, knitted fabrics, plastic extrusion along with misc. imports of goods.

We are among leading manufacturers of plastic items in Nepal with our brand name BAGMATI Super Strong TM standing for quality and assurity to all our customers, dealers and end consumers. We have a strong dealer network throughout Nepal and eastern part of India and our products reach to almost every household at their nearest point of purchase.
